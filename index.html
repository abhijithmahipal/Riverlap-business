Discover Riverlap Homestay

A hidden gem in Kerala where nature meets comfort, and every moment becomes a cherished memory.

Riverlap Homestay is more than just a place to stayâ€”it's an experience. Located in the heart of Nileshwar, Kerala, our traditional villa is set amidst coconut groves, backwaters, and a natural pond. The property is a short walk from a pristine, deserted beach, offering guests the perfect balance of relaxation and adventure.

Our homestay features three spacious villas, each designed to provide comfort and privacy while maintaining the authentic charm of Kerala architecture. Whether you're enjoying a homemade Kerala meal, exploring the backwaters on a canoe, or simply relaxing in a hammock by the water, Riverlap Homestay promises an unforgettable stay.
